About Us

Department of Information Technology

The Department of Information Technology at CSPIT was established in 1999 with the aim to develop professionals with computing, mathematical and business skills who can analyze, design and implement Information Technology solutions.

The department has state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty members dedicated to providing quality education and research opportunities to students.
