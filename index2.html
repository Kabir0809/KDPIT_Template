About CSPIT

Chandubhai S Patel Institute of Technology (CSPIT) was established in the year 2000, with a vision to develop world-class technical professionals who can deliver innovative technological solutions to meet the present and future needs of the society.

The institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines including Information Technology, Computer Science, and Electronics & Communication.
