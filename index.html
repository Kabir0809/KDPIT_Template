About Us

Department of Information Technology

Established in 2000, the Department of Information Technology has been at the forefront of technological education and research. We offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to produce industry-ready professionals.

Our curriculum is regularly updated to reflect the latest technological trends and industry requirements. The department has well-equipped laboratories, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections.
